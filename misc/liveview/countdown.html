Event Details

This Sunday (18th January, 2009), beginning at 9:00AM, the UNSW Solar Racing Team's record holding Sunswift III will take a lap of honour around greater Sydney and across the Harbour Bridge finishing in the botanical gardens (QE II Gate) at around 11am. It is your last chance to catch Sunswift III on the streets and get a sneak preview of the team's new vehicle design. A live feed with route, present location, telemetry data (including speed, power usage, solar panel output, etc) and a webcam image will be available right here on this site. For more information about the team, see www.sunswift.com.
